Quick Ground Rules

  • Keep it private. Student locations and community data stay within our cohort. No sharing, screenshotting, or forwarding to anyone outside the group.
  • Be respectful. Do not use any information here to contact people in ways that are unwelcome or outside the spirit of our community.
  • No guarantees. Class locations, business hours, and other details change. Contributor recommendations reflect personal experiences, not endorsements. When in doubt, verify independently.
  • Access is revocable. EWSS leadership may revoke access at any time.
  • Access is logged. Your IP address and access time are recorded for security. Misuse may result in revoked access.
